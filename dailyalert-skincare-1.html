What the data tells you before you spend a dollar on ads
Signal 01 — Proven Winner
K-Beauty Devices + Serums
Medicube and related K-beauty pages have run continuously for 816–1,130 days. The winning formula is a glow device bundled with a collagen serum. Extremely validated, but highly brand-dependent — difficult to white-label directly.
Signal 02 — Biggest Opportunity
At-Home LED / Red Light Facial Devices
Botanique Paris (500d), Kelly Moore (82d), Dermawell (76d), and iRESTORE (37d) are all selling variations of the same product: an at-home LED or red-light device for anti-aging and skin tightening. Multiple brands, same supplier source — prime dropship opportunity.
Signal 03 — Adjacent Opportunity
IPL Hair Removal Devices
Ulike (53d, 3 ad variants) and JOVS (20d) are actively spending on IPL hair removal. High AOV ($200–400), strong summer seasonality, well-stocked on Zendrop. Complements a beauty device store well as a second SKU.
