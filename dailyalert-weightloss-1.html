Signal 01 — Strongest Opportunity
At-Home Body Sculpting Devices
Christy Matis / Sculpiflex has run for 255 days selling a dynamic cupping + body sculpting device. High AOV ($100–250), strong visual before/after creative, and no prescription required. Clear dropship candidate — search "body sculpting cavitation device" on Zendrop.
Signal 02 — Chronic Condition Funnels
Community Page → Product Store Pattern
StepNura (185d), Parkinson Recovery Community (103d), and Lymphedema Support Group (26d) all use an empathetic community/support Facebook page to funnel sufferers into a health device store. The page looks like a support group — the product is the solution. High-converting pattern.
Signal 03 — Gut Health Emerging
Digestive Health / Probiotic Products
The Healthy Gut Journal (38d, get.lanural.com) uses an intensely personal story hook about chronic constipation. Gut health supplements are high-margin, repurchasable, and well-stocked on Zendrop. Strong second SKU candidate alongside a primary device offer.
